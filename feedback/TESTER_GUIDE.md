# REI Gear Advisor — Tester Guide

## Access

Open your browser and go to:

```
http://localhost:8501
```

Ask whoever is running the testing session for the URL if it's hosted remotely.

---

## First time: who are you?

You'll see a short onboarding screen. Enter your **name** and pick your **role**
(Gear Specialist / Developer / Product Manager / Other). Hit **Start chatting**.

---

## Chatting

Type a message as if you were a customer shopping for gear. For example:

> *"I need a sleeping bag for winter camping in the Rockies. Budget around $200."*

The agent will either ask a follow-up or give you a product recommendation.

---

## Rating (the only thing that's required)

After every agent response you'll see **👍** and **👎** buttons.

- Click one before sending your next message — the input is locked until you do.
- That's it. One click is all we need.

---

## If something was wrong (optional)

After clicking 👎, an expandable section appears:

1. **What went wrong?** — pick the closest option:
   - *Intent* — it misunderstood what kind of question you were asking
   - *Context* — it missed something you already told it
   - *Products* — the products shown don't match your query
   - *Response* — the products were fine but the text was wrong
   - *Nothing specific*

2. **What should it have said?** — free text, optional.

3. **Product relevance** — if products were shown, rate each one
   (Not relevant / Relevant / Perfect match), optional.

Hit **Submit** when done. You can skip this entirely.

---

## Ending the session

When you're done, click **End conversation** in the left sidebar.
You'll get an optional 1–5 overall rating. Submit it or just close the tab.

---

## Tips

- Try different query styles — vague, detailed, with budget, without.
- Multi-turn works: start vague, then add detail in follow-up messages.
- Safety scenarios are interesting: try high-risk activities (mountaineering, ice climbing).
- If the agent asks an unnecessary follow-up when you already gave the info, that's a useful 👎.
